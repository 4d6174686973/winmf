# ELF V2

[ELF V2](https://www.four-audio.com/en/products/elf) is a biaxial rotating arm produced by [Four Audio](https://www.four-audio.com/en/home/) in Aachen, which has been specifically developed for measuring balloon data of loudspeakers. It is controlled via RS485 port or using a USB to RS485 converter. ELF V2 can take loudspeakers weighing up to 100kg and with a maximum dimension of 1.5m.
