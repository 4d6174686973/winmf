# DA only

``DA only`` is used for playback on the activated output channels.

In the [Measurement setup](/en/help/adda/measurement-setup) the basic configuration is set.

In the [Exciter setup](/en/help/adda/exciter-setup) section the signal, which shall be played back, and the playback level are to be specified.

The [Amplifier setup](/en/help/adda/amplifier-setup) contains parameters of power amplifiers to be used.

``OK`` launches playback, ``Cancel`` quits the menu.
